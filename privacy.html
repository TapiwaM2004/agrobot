🌱 AgroBot Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

AgroBot is a WhatsApp-based AI assistant developed by TM AGRO Solutions to help smallholder farmers in Zimbabwe with agricultural advice, crop disease detection, soil health analysis, weather guidance, and marketplace services.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with AgroBot, we may collect:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Messages you send to the bot
  • Marketplace listings you post (item, location, price, contact number)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide agricultural advice and recommendations
  • Display your marketplace listings to other farmers
  • Improve the quality of our AI responses

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Marketplace listing contact numbers are shared with other AgroBot users as part of the buy/sell feature. AI queries are processed through Groq AI services.

5. Data Retention

Marketplace listings are stored until you request removal. Conversation data is not permanently stored.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Remove your marketplace listings
  • Stop using the service at any time
